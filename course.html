The Liminal Space Starter

Three modules to explore entrepreneurship through guided play

Module 1: Discover the Entrepreneur in Me

Learn about the liminal space concept - a threshold zone between your current state and potential entrepreneurial state. Understand why you can't "reason your way" into entrepreneurship and how play becomes the tool for exploration.

What You'll Learn:

  • What the liminal space is and why it matters
  • Why traditional reasoning fails for entrepreneurship decisions
  • How play becomes your exploration tool
  • The difference between slow and fast learning
  • Foundation for self-awareness ‚Üí business awareness

Module 2: Play + Breaking Frames

Experience real-world play through the Paperclipping exercise. Learn to distinguish between play (doing, learning, progressing) and pretending. Break frames that limit your thinking and discover what entrepreneurship would actually be like.

What You'll Do:

  • Complete the Paperclipping exercise (real-world play)
  • Experience the "stunning" moment of what's possible
  • Learn to break limiting frames
  • Understand play vs. pretending
  • Reflect on what you discovered about yourself

Module 3: Self-awareness ‚Üí Business awareness

Translate your self-awareness insights into business awareness. Reflect on your play experiences and understand how personal discoveries inform entrepreneurial decisions. Begin to see both your current life and potential entrepreneurial life together.

What You'll Discover:

  • How to translate self-awareness into business insights
  • The breakthrough moment: "Oh, that's what it would be like"
  • Seeing both states together (current + entrepreneurial)
  • Decision framework: What path is right for you?
  • Next steps for your entrepreneurial exploration
